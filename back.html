Two Ladies Walk Into a Box

December 7, 2016
by Eric Sherred | Staff

THE STORY

Good morning and welcome to the Morning Chalk Up. Today’s edition was chalked up while watching teachers at Kansas City High School tell individual students how special they are.

IS THIS A JOKE?

Sadly, no. They’ve already knocked up five affiliates so far: Copperhead CrossFit, Sol CrossFit, Spark CrossFit, 8th Street CrossFit and Back Alley CrossFit. While just about every affiliate we’ve been to felt very safe, like being with your family, it might be a good idea to double check the batteries on your security cams.

HOW DOES THE HEIST GO DOWN?

Two ladies walk into the box. One of them asks questions about joining the affiliate while the one with the baby sits down and grabs what she can quickly find.

HAVE THEY CAUGHT THESE BANDITS YET?

Authorities are still on the hunt, but the good news is that they were caught on a security camera so it’s only a matter of time.
